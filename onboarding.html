Two blocking modes
🔒
Strict Mode
Sites are blocked at the network level. The page never loads. Chrome shows an error — no way around it.

Best for: Anyone serious about staying off gambling sites.
⚠️
Soft Mode
A warning overlay appears before the site loads. You can choose to go back or continue anyway.

Best for: Accountability with flexibility — or testing the extension.

Quick start
1
You're already protected
Strict mode is on by default. Just close this tab and keep browsing — blocked sites will fail to load automatically.
2
Click the shield icon to open the popup
See your streak, today's block count, and total domains protected. Switch modes or update the blocklist from there.
3
Add custom sites if needed
Open the popup → Settings → Block domain. Paste any URL you want blocked. You can also whitelist specific sites you trust.
4
Keep your streak going
Every day you keep protection active, your streak grows. Don't break the chain.
